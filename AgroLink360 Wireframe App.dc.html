How it works

1List produce
Farmer uploads crop, quantity & price — in the app, or by USSD *920*36# with no internet.
2AI matches
The engine finds nearby buyers, predicts the best price and books shared transport.
3Track delivery
Live GPS from farm gate to buyer's door, with SMS updates for both sides.
4Get paid
MoMo escrow releases instantly on confirmed delivery. No middlemen.
